Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit this website (the "Site").

Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. Additionally, as you browse the Site, we collect information about the individual web pages that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Site, and information about how you interact with the Site. We refer to this automatically-collected information as "Device Information."

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • Cookies – Data files that are placed on your device and often include an anonymous unique identifier.
  • Log files – Track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  • Tracking pixels – Electronic files used to record information about how you browse the Site (e.g., TikTok Pixel).

Third-Party Tracking (TikTok Pixel)

This Site uses the TikTok Pixel, a tracking technology provided by TikTok Inc. The TikTok Pixel collects data about your visit to this Site, including page views and interactions, for the purpose of measuring advertising effectiveness and delivering targeted advertisements. This data is shared with TikTok in accordance with their privacy policy.

You can learn more about TikTok's data practices at: TikTok Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Information

We use the Device Information that we collect to:

  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
  • Optimize and improve our Site content and user experience
  • Analyze aggregate usage trends

Sharing Your Information

We share your personal information with third parties to help us use your information as described above. Specifically:

  • We use TikTok Pixel to measure advertising effectiveness. You can opt out of TikTok's targeted advertising at: TikTok Privacy Settings
  • When you click our affiliate links, you will be directed to ClickBank.com, which has its own privacy policy governing how they collect and use your information.

Your Rights

If you are a resident of certain jurisdictions, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us using the information below.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. All tracking data is collected and retained by TikTok according to their data retention policies.

Do Not Track

We do not alter our Site's data collection and use practices when we see a Do Not Track signal from your browser.

Children's Privacy

This Site is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
